Quick Cast

"Quick Cast" is a mechanic exploration prototype. I like exploring different game mechanics and this is one I've been wanting to use in a future game! The goal is a high apm experience where the player is constantly trying to do everything he can to survive. Inspired by characters of famous mobas like DotA 2's Invoker or LoL's Hwei, the player has to make the correct combination of patterns in order to defeat their enemies!

What I Worked On:

I am the solo developer of this game, every single thing in it was made by me! :D

Project Info:

  • Technologies - Unity
  • Languages - C#
  • Project Management - Git

Game released on Itch.io.
